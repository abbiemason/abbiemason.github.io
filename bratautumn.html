Brat summer continues

The Sweat Tour ft. Troye Sivan

Charli XCX is celebrating the end of Brat summer — and gearing up for Sweat tour Autumn! On Labor Day Monday, Charli XCX bid farewell to summer 2024, which was musically defined by her iconic, chartreuse-colored pop album. “Goodbye forever brat summer,” Charli wrote on X. Following Brat’s success, Rolling Stone declared that “the world had finally caught up” with the singer.Sweat is a co-headlining concert tour by English singer-songwriter Charli XCX and Australian singer-songwriter Troye Sivan, in support of their albums Brat (2024) and Something to Give Each Other (2023), respectively. The tour began on September 14, 2024, in Detroit, Michigan, and is currently set to conclude on October 23, 2024, in Seattle, Washington. English singer and DJ Shygirl will serve as the opening act. Charli XCX and Troye Sivan first collaborated in 2018 with their song "1999". Due to the song's popularity, the duo would collaborate further, releasing "2099" the following year. Sivan released his third studio album, Something to Give Each Other, on October 13, 2023,while Charli XCX released her sixth studio album Brat on June 7, 2024.In the summer of 2024, both artists embarked on separate tours in support of the albums. On April 17, 2024, the duo announced Sweat, a joint tour of North America.

Special guests

XCX and Sivan brought their co-headlining ‘Sweat’ tour to Madison Square Garden in New York. In past editions of the tour, the two musicians have had a habit of bringing out special guest performers to join them onstage for several songs. For this go around, Addison Rae and Lorde joined Charli and Troye. Addison Rae arrived onstage first, joining Charli XCX for the remix of ‘Von Dutch’. Rae then performed her own song, ‘Diet Pepsi’ with Charli and Troye beside her. Later on in the night, Lorde surprised fans in attendance by coming out on stage dressed very similarly to Charli XCX. Together, the two performed their remix of ‘Girl, so confusing’.
