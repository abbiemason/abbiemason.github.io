a guide to brat summer

read time: 5 mins

"Brat summer" refers to a casual, carefree approach to summer living that’s all about embracing a laid-back, rebellious, and self-indulgent attitude. It's not about being an actual brat, but rather taking the summer to focus on yourself, your desires, and doing what makes you happy. Here’s a short guide to making the most of your brat summer. 1. Embrace Comfort and Style Summer is all about feeling good in your own skin. Choose outfits that are both comfortable and stylish, like oversized tees, crop tops, and your favorite sneakers or sandals. Let your personal style shine without worrying too much about what's "in" or "trendy." 2. Do What You Want The key to brat summer is living unapologetically. Want to spend the whole day at the beach, binge-watch a show, or skip a workout? Go for it! It’s about following your whims and doing what brings you joy without guilt. 3. Indulge in Your Favorite Foods Whether it’s ice cream, BBQ, or a favorite snack, allow yourself to indulge in the foods you love. Brat summer is about enjoying yourself without overthinking calories or strict diets. It’s all about balance and savoring the moment. 4. Set Boundaries Brat summer doesn’t mean being rude, but it does mean setting firm boundaries. If you want to take a break from social obligations or need alone time, say so. You get to decide how you spend your time and with whom. 5. Be Playful and Spontaneous Don’t overplan your summer days. Be open to last-minute adventures, whether it’s a road trip, a pool party, or an impromptu hangout with friends. Let spontaneity lead the way! 6. Confidence is Key Ultimately, brat summer is about feeling confident in yourself and your choices. Don’t let others' opinions bring you down. Rock your confidence in everything you do, and enjoy the summer with a carefree attitude.
